About Me

My name is Arnav Mehta, Im a indian immigrant, that gained a love a food from my father. Watching him in the kitchen cook, made me always curious about different foods. With my father, i travelled to various restraunts and places to eat. Driving 2 hours for dinner or getting a reservation 3 months in advance. If it means good food, we would go.
